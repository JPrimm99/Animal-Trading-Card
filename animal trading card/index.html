Labrador Retriever

Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog.
    Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Familiaris
    Average Weight: 64-79lbs
    Avgerage Lifespan: 10-12 years
    Temperament: Outgoing, Kind, and Trusting

Labrador was developed in the UK of Newfoundland,and was named after the Labrador region of that colony.
